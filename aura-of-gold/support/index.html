Frequently Asked Questions

Is Aura of Gold free to play?
Yes — Aura of Gold is completely free. There are no in-app purchases, no subscriptions, and no hidden costs.
Do I need an account or internet connection?
No account or login is required. The app works fully offline once downloaded.
How long is the story?
The game is designed to be enjoyed at your own pace. You can play in short sessions or explore it all in one sitting — there's no timer or pressure.
Do my choices matter?
Yes. The choices you make throughout the story shape Elara's journey — where she goes, who she meets, and who she becomes.
The app won't open / keeps crashing. What can I do?
Try restarting your device and reopening the app. If the issue persists, delete and reinstall the app. If it still doesn't work, please contact us — we'd love to help.
