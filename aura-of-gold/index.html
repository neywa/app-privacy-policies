Aura of Gold

Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 4, 2026  ·  Last updated: April 4, 2026

1 Introduction

Aura of Gold is a healing story game developed by an independent developer. This Privacy Policy explains what data the App collects, why it collects it, and how it is handled.

We are committed to protecting your privacy. The short version: we do not collect or store any personal data — period. By using the App you agree to the practices described below.

2 What Data We Collect

Game Progress & Settings

The App saves your story progress, choices, and preferences so you can continue where you left off.

All of this data is stored locally on your device only. It is never transmitted to us or any third party.

What We Do NOT Collect

  • Name or email address
  • Location data
  • Device or advertising IDs
  • Usage analytics
  • Crash reports
  • Camera or microphone
  • Contacts
  • Account credentials

The App does not require you to create an account, sign in, or provide any personal information at any point.

3 Third-Party Services

Aura of Gold does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, tracking, or crash-reporting SDKs. No data is shared with any external service.

The App operates fully offline and makes no network requests of any kind.

4 Data Storage & Retention

We do not operate any servers or databases. All app data — story progress, choices, and settings — is stored locally on your device only. This data is not accessible to us and is never transmitted anywhere.

Uninstalling the App permanently removes all locally stored data from your device.

5 Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13 (or under 16 in the European Union). We do not knowingly collect any personal data from children. Since the App collects no data whatsoever, there is no risk of inadvertent collection from minors.

6 Your Rights

Since we do not collect or store personal data on our end, there is no data held by us to access, correct, or delete. All data remains on your device and is fully under your control. You can remove all data at any time by uninstalling the App.

7 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
