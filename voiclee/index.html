Privacy Policy

Voiclee – Voice Reminder App

Last updated: April 3, 2025

Short version: Voiclee does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers. Everything stays on your device.

1. About This Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Voiclee ("the app", "we", "us") handles information when you use the application on Android or iOS. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about how the app works.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

Voiclee does not collect, store, share, or transmit any of the following:

  • Voice recordings or audio data
  • Reminder content or text
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Usage statistics or analytics
  • Location data
  • Account credentials or personal profile information

We do not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs.

3. Microphone Permission (RECORD_AUDIO)

Voiclee requests access to your device's microphone solely for the purpose of capturing your voice so you can create reminders by speaking. This is the core functionality of the app.

  • Audio is processed entirely on your device using the operating system's built-in speech recognition.
  • When you attach a voice note to a reminder, the recording is saved locally on your device only. It is never sent to any server or shared with any third party.
  • Voice notes are only created when you explicitly choose to record one — the microphone is never used in the background.
  • Deleting a reminder also permanently deletes any voice note attached to it.

You can revoke microphone permission at any time through your device's system settings. Without microphone access, the voice input feature will not be available, but you can still manage existing reminders manually.

4. Data Storage

All reminders, voice notes, and app preferences are stored locally on your device using standard platform storage (Android: local app storage; iOS: local app storage). Voice note audio files remain on your device and are deleted permanently when you delete the associated reminder or uninstall the app. This data is not accessible to us and is never transmitted anywhere.

5. Children's Privacy

Voiclee is not directed at children under the age of 13 (or under 16 in the European Union). We do not knowingly collect any data from children. Since the app collects no data at all, there is no risk of inadvertent collection from minors.

6. Changes to This Policy

If we make significant changes to this privacy policy — for example, if we add new features that involve data collection — we will update this page and revise the "Last updated" date above. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
